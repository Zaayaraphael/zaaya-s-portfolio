# Company Logos Instructions

Place company logos here with specific names:

## Required Files:
- `uootes-logo.png` - Uootes company logo

## Recommended Specifications:
- **Format**: PNG (with transparency preferred) or SVG
- **Size**: 200x100px or similar rectangular format
- **Background**: Transparent or white
- **Quality**: High resolution, clear and professional

## Where they appear:
- Companies section (horizontal layout with equal spacing)
- Grayscale filter with color on hover
- Responsive grid on mobile devices

## Example:
Save the Uootes logo as: `public/images/companies/uootes-logo.png`

## Adding More Companies:
1. Add new company data in `src/data/portfolio-data.ts`
2. Save logo as `public/images/companies/company-name-logo.png`
3. Update the logo path in the data file
